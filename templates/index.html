What do I do instead?!

If you're an Elive user who came from Windows or macOS, that might be a question you've been asking yourself all day.

What Do I Do Instead is a website that will (hopefully) help! Simply enter your search query and it will attempt to search for programs regarding that search.

I hope you enjoy What Do I Do Instead?!!

I'm always looking for contributions. If you can, go to the GitHub repo.
